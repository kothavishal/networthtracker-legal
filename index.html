Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Net Worth Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information that you voluntarily provide in the app:

  • Financial information such as income, net worth, assets, liabilities, and savings

We do not collect personal identifiers unless you explicitly provide them.

2. Data Storage

All financial data you enter is stored either:

  • Locally on your device, or

We do not store passwords or banking credentials.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Net worth tracking and visualization

  • Tax and retirement contribution estimates
  • Projections and scenario modeling

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your financial data with third parties except:

  • When required to operate app services (e.g., analytics or crash reporting tools)

5. Tracking

We do not use your data for tracking across apps or websites.

6. Financial Disclaimer

All insights (including tax savings, retirement projections, and net worth estimates) are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice.

7. Data Deletion

You may delete your data at any time by removing the app or contacting us at the email below.
