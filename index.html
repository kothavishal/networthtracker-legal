Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Net Worth Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information You Provide

The app allows you to voluntarily enter financial information, including:

  • Income and W-2 details
  • Net worth (assets and liabilities)
  • Retirement contributions (including 401(k))
  • Tax-related estimates and financial goals

2. Data Storage

All personal financial data is stored locally on your device.

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal financial information on our servers.

3. Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance and stability.

This data:

  • Does not include financial information
  • Does not identify you personally
  • Is used only for app performance and feature improvement

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We do not share your personal financial data because it is not transmitted off your device.

Anonymous analytics data may be processed by third-party tools used for performance monitoring.

5. Tracking

We do not track users across apps, websites, or services.

6. Financial Disclaimer

All insights provided by the app (including tax estimates, retirement projections, and net worth analysis) are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice.

7. Data Deletion

Since all financial data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app.

8. Security

We rely on your device’s built-in security mechanisms to protect locally stored data. No system is completely secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
